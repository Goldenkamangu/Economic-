📱🔌 Complementary goods

Complementary goods are products and services that are used together, meaning the demand for one is directly linked to the demand for the other.

☕🍵 Substitute goods

Substitute goods are products that consumers can use in place of one another to satisfy the same need or want. When the price of one good rises, demand for its substitute typically increases as consumers switch to the cheaper alternative.
